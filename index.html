About Me

Hi! I am Tanmoy Munshi currently pursuing Engineering in Electronics and Communication Engineering. I am highly passionate and having great interest to learn new things. Having a great interest in IoT (Internet of Things) automation tech, Data Science, Arduino projects, Coding, Research. I am having huger to work on projects. My hobbies are photography (specially having inclination to nature and aviation photography), blogging, coding, travelling and listening to musics. I love to solve real-life problems.
I have participated in several hackathons and won quite a few of them. One of them was a National Level Hackathon Bengalathon 2019-20 organized by the Government of West Bengal and Nasscom. Where my team was the National Level Winner (1/139) and I was the team lead. Me and my team won the first prize with Rs 5 Lakh cash award for furthur development of the project. We solved one of the unsolved real-life problem faced by paralysis patients. This product help paralysis patient to move with the help of there eye movement. The product which we have presented was Patented in Sep 2019. I was invited as a speaker in Health Tech 3.0 organized by Medica Hospital and Bengal Chamber of Commerce and Industry (BCC&i) presented product. And participated in some of the hackathons like SIH2020, Conquer COVID 19, etc. And not to mention but I am a foodie. And I believe in smart work and working smart is the only goal to achieve efficiency in any work or project.
